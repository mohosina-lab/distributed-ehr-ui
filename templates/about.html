About Us

Distributed EHR is a course project demonstrating a client UI that interacts with backend services for patient record management. Doctors can create and update records, while patients can view their own information after authentication.

Roles
  • Doctor: Create patients, update medical data, access records (with logging).
  • Patient: View personal records and update non-critical fields.
